An indepth look at the 'Clone Wars' project

Over four instalments of their series, Show Dem camp - made up of Ghost (Olumide Ayeni) and Tec (Wale Davies) - have cemented their unique sonic footprint as master blenders of high-life, R&B, Afrobeats, pop and alte with Hip-Hop and lyrical concious story telling.
"The clone Wars series was born out of the fact that we felt everything was sounding the same," Tec tells Apple Music. When you look wider into Nigerian society,theres a lot of clones in general, whether we're following constructs created by Tribalism, Religion or Money. So Clone Wars for us as it developed, became less about the Music Industry being clones but observing all the clones and clone behaviour in our society".

Created against the backdrop of a 2020 spent in lockdown, Clone Wars Vol.5 - The Algorhythm takes that story forward with an honest reflection of how we consume a never-ending stream of similar content.
With a nod to their favourite albums of the 90's and early 2000's, Ghost and Tec punctuate with light-hearted skits that tend to shift the energy between the heavy themes they explore, from self discovery ('Align') and embracing independence ('Rise of the Underdogs')to speaking truth to power and understanding the collective power of the people('Ghost Rant').
